Content:
We analyzed 200+ B2B SaaS LinkedIn Ad accounts.
The #1 cold campaign killer isn't budget, targeting, or the platform.

It's that most teams launch with "creative" ads.

Sounds backwards right? 

Here's what the data shows:
The best-performing cold ads across our entire client base share the same thing.. they're boring. Clear, direct, straightforward.

→ What you do → The pain you solve → Why you're different

That's it. 3 main angles. 
Works in virtually every B2B account we've managed.

The teams that try to get clever with cold creative - abstract visuals, witty taglines, "brand-first" messaging - consistently underperform the ones that just state the obvious clearly.

Your cold audience doesn't know you yet. They don't need to be impressed. They need to understand what you do in 3 seconds.

That's one of three mistakes I break down in this week's newsletter. The other two:
→ The ad format 85% of advertisers aren't using (it builds warm retargeting pools faster than anything else)
→ Why your exec team doesn't care about your CPM reports and what to show them instead
