The big "secret" about the people grinding 24/7 is that they'll regret it.

They won't tell you that, of course.

And the comments will be filled with people defending a life of non-stop work.

They're going to change the world.
They enjoy spending all day at their job.
They are going to be wealthy beyond belief.

But the *actual* truth is a lot different.

How do I know?

Because I've lived it many times over.
Because most of my friends have done the same.

And no matter how much you enjoy work, how much money you make, what kind of impact you create, and how famous and adored you are...

Life sucks if you're neglecting your friends, family, and community.

So for those of you out there grinding because you think someone else's life is worth living...

Don't build someone else's idea of a great life.

Build your own.

I send one short note every Saturday on work, money, and a life built on purpose, to help you do that.
